Privacy Policy

Developer: Trivia Royale Team - Effective: March 29, 2026 - Contact: hastagtamilnadu@gmail.com

Information We Collect

If you sign in with Google, Trivia Royale may collect account information such as your display name, email address, profile photo, and account identifier. We also process gameplay progress and in-app data stored on your device, plus manual redeem request details when you choose to submit a gift card request.

How We Use Information

We use this information to operate your account, support gameplay, show local ranking progress, process validated coin balances, manual gift card requests, and protect the security and integrity of the app.

Manual Redeem Requests

When you request a gift card reward, we store your user ID, display name, email address, selected gift card type, requested reward value, coin amount, request status, and request timestamp in Firebase so the request can be reviewed manually. There is no instant payout. Redeem request request records may be retained for up to 12 months for audit and fraud review purposes.

Device Storage

Gameplay progress and other in-app data may be stored locally on your device to support your experience and keep the app functional between sessions.

What We Do Not Collect

Trivia Royale does not collect payment card details, bank account information, your contacts, or precise location for the reward request flow described above.

Sharing

We use Firebase services to support authentication, app data, validated coin checks, and manual redeem request processing. We do not sell your personal information. Information may be disclosed only when required to comply with applicable law or protect the app against abuse.

Security

We use encryption in transit, authentication controls, and restricted access measures designed to protect user information and connected app features.

Updates

This policy may change over time as features evolve. Continued use of Trivia Royale after updates means the latest version of this policy will apply.
